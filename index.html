About Meridian

Meridian is a modern mobile-first banking demo built to showcase a clean, secure, and accessible banking experience. It focuses on clarity, fast transactions, and meaningful financial insights — designed for everyday users and developers exploring banking UX patterns.

Built as a front-end simulation, Meridian demonstrates account flows, transfers, savings goals, and in-app support. It is intended for design and development purposes and does not represent a live financial product.
